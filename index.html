How to Play

  • Move with Left/Right (←/→ or A/D). Jump with Space (or ↑). On mobile, use buttons.
  • At checkpoints, the game pauses and shows a question.
  • Three bricks A / B / C appear above you. Jump to hit the brick you choose.
  • If you don’t answer within a few seconds, the game auto-continues (logged as no-answer).
  • At the end, download CSV for analysis.
