How to Play

  • First, the game pauses so you can read the question properly.
  • Then aim and launch the glowing ball into lane A, B, or C.
  • Keyboard: ← / → to aim. Hold Space to charge. Release Space to launch.
  • Mobile: tap/drag on the board to aim. Hold the “Hold & Release” button to charge, then release.
  • Correct lane: +10. Wrong lane: 0. One launch per question.
